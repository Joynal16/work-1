Frequently Asked Questions

Can’t find what you’re looking for? Reach out to our support team.

How does the 14-day free trial work?
You get full access to all premium features for two weeks. No credit card is required to start. At the end of the trial, you can choose a plan that fits your needs or stay on the free tier.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription from your dashboard settings instantly. You will retain access to your paid features until the end of your current billing cycle.

Do you offer volume discounts for large teams?
Absolutely! For teams of 20 or more, we offer custom enterprise pricing. Contact our sales department for a personalized quote.
